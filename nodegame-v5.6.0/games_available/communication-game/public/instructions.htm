Instructions for communication-game

Please read them carefully

This is an experiment in the economics of decision-making. You will earn money depending on the decisions you make. Your earnings will be paid to you at the end of the experiment. At the beginning of the experiment, you will be randomly paired with another human participant. You may have to wait some time for another MTurker to be recruited. You and your pair will then participate in the experiment, which consists of two steps.

We first describe the second step so that you know what decisions you will be making and how your earnings will be determined. Then we describe the first step of the experiment in which you can send a message to your pair.

Second Step

In this step you and your pair will separately and independently select an option in secret. Both of you will only have two options to choose from: Option A and Option B. You and your pairâ€™s decisions will be combined. Based upon your combined decisions, you and your pair will be able to earn money. There are four possible situations:

  • If both of you choose OPTION A, then both of you will earn $1.00.
  • If you choose OPTION A and your pair chooses OPTION B, then you will earn $0.20 and your pair will earn $0.90.
  • If you choose OPTION B and your pair chooses OPTION A, then you will earn $0.90 and your pair will earn $0.20.
  • If both of you choose OPTION 2, then both of you will earn $0.50.

Now, we describe the first step.

First Step

In the first step, you and your pair will send a message to each other. Both of you can choose which message to send through a screen that looks like this.

You may be given the option to send additional messages. These additional messages, if any, will be added to the end of all the other messages to form one long message. Once both players have decided on their message, you will receive your pair's message and then proceed to the second step.

Make sure to understand the instructions correctly as when you press the "DONE" button, you will proceed to a comprehension quiz. You must answer the quiz correctly to play the game.
